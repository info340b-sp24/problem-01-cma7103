Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is a fictional character from Japanese manga One piece. He is the second member of the Straw Hat Pirates and the first to join. He was born in the East China Sea and is also known as the "Pirate Hunter". As a Master of the Three Sword Style, his dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world.
